Degree Workshop 1

Pharmacy Inventory Management System

Engineered a robust Inventory Management System written entirely in C++. The architecture handles complex data structures for real-time tracking of medical supplies, expiration dates, and automated low-stock alerts. Focused on memory efficiency, clean algorithmic design, and a user-friendly console interface for pharmacy staff.

Self Project

Brain Tumor Detection

Developed an Artificial Intelligence model designed to automatically detect and classify brain tumors from MRI scans. This project involved training deep learning algorithms on medical imaging datasets to achieve high accuracy, demonstrating the potential of AI in assisting medical diagnostics and improving healthcare outcomes.

Computer Vision Project

LaborLens

Engineered "LaborLens," a specialized computer vision system for cafes. The software tracks and detects customers in the shop while monitoring barista workflow, specifically counting the number of cups produced by each barista during their shift. This provides valuable operational analytics for staffing and efficiency management.
